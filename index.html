The International Conference on Advances in Computing (ICAiC) 2025 is a prestigious event organized by REC, Jaipur,India. This conference is dedicated to exploring significant developments in the field of computing, with a focus on cutting-edge advancements. The ICAiC-2024 aims to unite researchers, academicians, industry professionals, and government representatives to discuss key topics such as intelligent computing, network and social computing, data and cloud computing, computer algorithms and applications, as well as system and software engineering. The conference offers a platform for presenting innovative research and fostering collaboration among experts from various domains. Scholars are encouraged to submit their latest research findings in these and other related areas, contributing to the collective advancement of computing technologies.
