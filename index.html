1st International Conference on Advances in Computing (ICAiC 2025)

July 4-5, 2025

Computer Science & Engineering, REC Jaipur, India

Computer Science & Engineering, REC Jaipur

Attending ICAiC-2025 will provide participants with numerous technical benefits that can significantly enhance their professional and academic pursuits. The conference offers a unique platform for researchers and industry professionals to present their latest findings, enabling them to receive valuable feedback from experts in their respective fields. This exchange of ideas fosters innovation and can lead to the refinement of ongoing research or the development of new research directions.

Participants will have the opportunity to network with peers and establish collaborations that could result in joint research projects, co-authored publications, or industrial partnerships. The diverse range of topics covered, from intelligent computing to system and software engineering, ensures that attendees will gain exposure to the latest advancements across multiple areas of computing.

Moreover, the conference's focus on cutting-edge developments ensures that attendees stay at the forefront of technological trends, equipping them with knowledge that can be applied to both academic and industry settings. By engaging with a global community of experts, participants will be better positioned to contribute to the advancement of computing technologies, enhancing their professional growth and impact in the field.
